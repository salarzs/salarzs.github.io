About Me

I'm a Fluid Mechanics Engineer passionate about solving complex flow problems through Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD), numerical simulations, and advanced mathematical modeling. I enjoy turning complicated fluid behaviors into clear, practical solutions, especially when working with multi-phase flows. My goal is always to bridge the gap between theoretical research and real-world applications, helping industries tackle fluid-related challenges efficiently and creatively.
